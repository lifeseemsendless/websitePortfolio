Our Story

Sixty Years
of Passion

Founded in the Swiss Alps by two visionary engineers, Velor has always believed that the perfect automobile is a work of art. Every vehicle we create bears the mark of relentless pursuit — of speed, of beauty, of the ineffable sensation of perfect motion.

1964
Founded in Geneva
Ernst Velor and Marco Aldini create their first prototype in a converted watch factory.
1978
First Production Model
The V8 Classico enters production, capturing hearts with its revolutionary twin-turbo engine.
1995
Formula Racing Entry
Velor Motorsport wins back-to-back championships, cementing our performance legacy.
2019
Electric Future Begins
Project EV-1 unveiled — a 1,000 hp all-electric hypercar that shocked the automotive world.
